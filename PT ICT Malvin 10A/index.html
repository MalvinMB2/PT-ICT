About Us

Mutiara Bangsa 2 School is a school under the auspices of the Ananda Bina Smart Foundation. Mutiara Bangsa began to be inscribed with gold ink for the first time in 2003 under the name Mutiara Bangsa 1 which is located in the Poris Indah housing complex, Tangerang. Under the auspices of the Mutiara Bangsa Foundation, this school uses the national curriculum as the basis for its teaching and learning activities. Due to the amount of support and trust from parents of students given to Mutiara Bangsa, in 2005 Mutiara Bangsa expanded its wings by building Mutiara Bangsa 3 Kindergarten, Elementary, Middle School, High School in Jelambar Barat III, West Jakarta. It didn't stop there, Mutiara Bangsa School continued its work in the world of education by building Mutiara Bangsa 2 in 2008 which is located at Jalan Husein Sastranegara no 29 B, Tangerang. Located at front of the Duta Garden residential complex, Tangerang.
